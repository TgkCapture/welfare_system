Our Mission

To foster financial stability and mutual support among MZUGOSS Class of 2018 members through systematic contributions and transparent welfare management.

  • Promote financial discipline
  • Ensure timely support during needs
  • Maintain transparency in all transactions
  • Build a sustainable welfare ecosystem

Our Vision

To create a self-sustaining welfare system that serves as a model for alumni associations, ensuring no member faces financial hardship alone.

  • 100% member participation
  • Digital-first approach
  • Real-time financial tracking
  • Community-driven growth
