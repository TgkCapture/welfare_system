Contribution Policy

Monthly Contributions

  • Amount: MWK 5,000 per member per month
  • Deadline: 5th of every month
  • Method: Mobile money or bank transfer
  • Receipt: Send proof of payment to treasurer

Payment Channels

Airtel Money: 0888 123 456
TNM Mpamba: 0999 123 456
Bank Transfer: Account details available

Late Payments

  • Late fee: MWK 1,000 after 5th of month
  • Maximum grace period: 10 days
  • After 15th: Considered defaulted for the month

Payout Policy

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a member in good standing for 6+ months
  • No outstanding contributions
  • Valid reason for payout request
  • Committee approval required

Payout Categories

Emergency Medical

Up to MWK 200,000

For critical medical situations
Education Support

Up to MWK 150,000

For educational advancement
Business Startup

Up to MWK 100,000

For entrepreneurial ventures

Application Process

  1. Submit written application to committee
  2. Provide supporting documents
  3. Committee review within 7 days
  4. Approval by majority vote
  5. Disbursement within 3 working days

Membership Policy

Eligibility

Decision Making

  • Simple majority for routine decisions
  • Two-thirds majority for constitutional changes
  • Emergency decisions by executive committee
  • All decisions documented and shared

Penalties & Compliance

Default Consequences

Stage 1: 1-2 Months Default
  • Formal warning notice
  • Late fee accumulation
  • Payment plan option
Stage 2: 3-6 Months Default
  • Suspension from payouts
  • Increased late fees
  • Committee review
Stage 3: 6+ Months Default
  • Membership suspension
  • Legal action consideration
  • Full arrears settlement required
