Contribution Guidelines

1. Late Payment Penalties

5% penalty per month for late contributions. Multiple months accumulate penalties.

Example: 2 months late = 10% penalty

2. Back Payments

Missed months must be paid for the specific period they were missed.

3. Benefits Calculation

Number of times you can receive help = Number of months you've contributed.

Example: 3 months contribution = 3 times eligible for support

Penalty Calculation Guide

Understanding how late payment penalties are calculated:

Penalty Rate: 5% per month

Formula: Penalty = Monthly Contribution × (5% × Number of Late Months)

Example Calculation:
Monthly Contribution: K1,000
2 months late: K1,000 × (5% × 2) = K1,000 × 10% = K100 penalty
Total to pay: K1,000 + K100 = K1,100
3 months late:
K1,000 × (5% × 3) = K1,000 × 15% = K150 penalty
Total to pay: K1,000 + K150 = K1,150

Benefits Eligibility Guide

How your contributions determine your support eligibility:

1:1 Benefit Ratio

Rule: Each month of contribution = One time eligible for support

Scenario 1:
6 months contributed = Eligible for support 6 times total
(Can be any combination of funeral, sickness, or wedding support)
Scenario 2:
2 months contributed = Eligible for support 2 times only
Once used, must contribute more to be eligible again
Important

Remember: Only those who contribute regularly are eligible for any welfare support. Non-contributors cannot receive any welfare money from the community fund.
